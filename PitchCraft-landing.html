How It Works

From brief to signed deal

Four steps. No fluff.

01
Load a template or start fresh
Pick from 19 industry presets or start from a blank brief. Quick-fill Presets auto-populate industry, tone, services, and the core challenge for you.
02
Fill your brief & run the audit
Add client details, competitors, previous work done, and your digital marketing audit findings — SEO, Meta, Google Ads, Email, Website, and reputation.
03
Generate your proposal instantly
